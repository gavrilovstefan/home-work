About me

Timco Gavrilov is born in Murtino, village near the city of Strumica. He finished his high school at the Art School "Lazar Licenoski" - Skopje. He continued with his education at Ss. "Cyril and Methodius" Faculty of Fine Arts - Skopje in the Sculpture Deparment, also learning the technique of conservation and restoration. He lives and work at his own atelier and has a job at the local company Cvejo (Arts & Entertainment) as a designer.
